We believe that quality starts at the tip of the needle: the cutters, stitching operators, the finishing staff and production supervisors, are all responsible in controlling and assuring quality. An entirely independent audit department makes sure that the products are being manufactured exactly as per given specifications. It has complete authority to pass or fail any consignment before presenting it to the final inspection.
