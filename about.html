Welcome to 7-Star Garments

Our History

7-Star Garments is one of Pakistan's largest manufactures and exporters of men's, ladies and children's woven garments. We started exports in 1991 and since then, have expanded our business to various countries in USA, Europe & Canada, these being our major markets.

By producing high quality products, making timely shipments and delivering efficient services, we have established a reputation of being committed to customers satisfaction.

Although our presence is global, our emphasis is on giving special attention to each client taking care to accurately determine individual requirements. Our products and services are customized to meet the needs of each client. Two decades in pursuit of perfection has firmly put our faith in the fact that successful management of any operation requires efficiency, administrative ability and dedication. For us at 7-Star Garments, however, the ultimate test of success lies in attaining customers satisfaction.

Our Mission

We know that our management is held together due to its strong roots, which we believe to be our employees. Their intermingling has nourished 7-Star Garments to Build up as a strong enterprise because we treat each of our employees as a partner.

In return we receive unequivocal and loyal support that allows us to serve our buyers effectively and with greater inspiration.

In order to serve the customer right, 7-Star Garments has a detailed system that starts from the customers order and ends at getting the goods shipped to the customers. Different departments in the factory interact and coordinate with each other to accomplish this goal.
